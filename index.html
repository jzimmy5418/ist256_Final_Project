FAQ

"What is the difference between materials offered?"

Resin prints offer a higher quality print with better details, however these prints are more fragile amd are not recommended for prints where strength is required. Meanwhile, standard filament 3d prints are generally stronger and offer a wider range of materials. On our site we offer PLA and ABS plastics. ABS is considered stronger and more heat-resistant while PLA is more environmentally friendly.

"Why does it take up to a week for the product to be shipped?"

All of our models are printed on demand, meaning they are only printed once an order is placed. This is to keep our products at the highest possible quality, as with resin prints there is the possibility of warping and becoming brittle.
