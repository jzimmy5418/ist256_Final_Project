FAQ

"What is the difference between materials offered?"
Resin prints offer a higher quality print with better details, however these prints are more fragile amd are not recommended for prints where strength is required. Meanwhile, standard filament 3d prints are generally stronger and offer a wider range of materials. On our site we offer PLA and ABS plastics. ABS is considered stronger and more heat-resistant while PLA is more environmentally friendly.

"Why does it take up to a week for the product to be shipped?"
All of our models are printed on demand, meaning they are only printed once an order is placed. This is to keep our products at the highest possible quality, as with resin prints there is the possibility of warping and becoming brittle.

"My print was damaged in shipping. Can I get a replacement?"
Yes we will cover a replacement for any product damaged in shipping. Please contact us at warranty@jz3dprints.com, including pictures of the product, and we will follow up within a business day and send you a replacement product.

"Do you offer your designs anywhere?"
Most of my designs currently available for purchase on this site are not available to download, however as these designs get periodically updated, the older versions will be available.

About Us

Our Goal: Provide users with quality scale models and services to make their ideas come to life.

What started out as a simple kit-bashing project for my model railroad when I was 14, has led to this company with many various scale models. I found it difficult to find scale models of one-off prototype locomotives and railcars. So with a little 3D printer and some basic design software, I started out creating models for my own projects. Today, after some feedback, I am proud to offer these designs for modelers to purchase.

Joshua Zimmerman
Founder

Information Sciences & Technology student at The Pennsylvania State University.
Interests in model railroading, fishing, and railroad communications/signaling.
